What is Vietnam Open Robotics Challenge Cần Thơ 2024

Vietnam Open Robotics Challenge Cần Thơ (VORC Cần Thơ) is a Robotics competition for high school students in the Mekong Delta. The competition is organized with the aim of bringing Robotics and STEAM education closer to students; inspire and build a sustainable STEAM community in Vietnam. Through the seasons from 2020 to now, VORC has gradually become a familiar playground and received great attention from students who are passionate about Robotics and STEAM nationwide.

Coming to 2024 season, Vietnam Open Robotics Challenge is officially organized in Cần Thơ with the theme: "Reviving water sources" to find the next inspired generation, bringing passion and spirit of STEAM and Robotics to the whole country. Not only that, the competition also emphasizes the spirit of cooperation, teamwork between young people.

VORC Cần Thơ 2024 Rules

Vietnam Open Robotics Challenge 2024 will be held in 2 rounds: Online training round and Challenge round.

Online training round:
Teams will participate in training programs on mechanical design, programming, and electronics. In addition, teams also have the opportunity to access and learn more about topics such as communication, team management, and interviews with the judges. At the end of the training process, teams submit their result to the organizers for evaluation.

Challenge round:
Top 16 teams with the highest ranking in the online training round will be sponsored with the K12 Maker kit to build a robot according to the theme of the competition and participate in the Challenge round at FPT Cần Thơ on 07/12/2024

Teams need to join Zalo VORC Cần Thơ 2024 to update the competition announcements, documents, and ask questions to the advisor team here
