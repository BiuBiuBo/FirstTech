FIRST Tech Challenge Vietnam 2023-2024

Volunteer Registration

FIRST® Tech Challenge is a global robotics competition for young people. The 2019-2020 season had nearly 700,000 participants from over 100 countries.

This year, FIRST Tech Challenge officially appears in Vietnam for the first time. Licensed by FIRST in the US, FIRST Tech Challenge Vietnam is a national technology competition to select the champion sponsored to participate in FIRST Tech Challenge in the US in 2024.

The theme of the FIRST Tech Challenge and FIRST Tech Challenge Vietnam competition: “CENTERSTAGE” - the role of STEM in the fields of art, creativity, entertainment, while responding to and enhancing awareness of the interdisciplinary nature of Art with the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

Volunteer Requirement

  • Fluent in English (reading and writing)
  • Above 18 years old/li>
  • Make a commitment to finish as volunteer through out the event from 24/02/2024
  • Make a commitment to participate in online training sessions from 02 - 18/01/2024 (about 3 hours/week)
  • Love to share knowledge and connect with people around the world
  • Having skills in robotics is an advantage (especially programming, mechanics, electronics), but not required

Volunteers must always behave based on the spirit of Gracious Professionalism, Kindness, honesty, and transparency. This is an international event that brings together many people from all over the world, so it is important to be polite and respectful to everyone.

Selection and training timeline for FTC VN 2023-2024 volunteers

Trách nhiệm:

Volunteer benefits:

  • Get trained on volunteer skills at the FIRST Tech Challenge event and other technical skills (depending on the role).
  • Get trained online and offline with the international FTC team from Vietnam, Australia, China, and India.
  • Chance to connect with FIRST and FIRST Tech Challenge global community.
  • Build relationships with experts, students passionate about robotics throughout the competition.
  • Improve professional skills, practical skills, and soft skills through real experience.
  • Get a volunteer certificate.
  • Contributing to the development and spread of the robotics community in Vietnam.
  • Understand and experience how to organize international robotics competitions in Vietnam.
