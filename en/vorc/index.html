What is Vietnam Open Robotics Challenge 2024 ?

Vietnam Open Robotics Challenge 2024 (VORC 2024) has returned with the topic “Reviving water sources”.

Vietnam Open Robotics Challenge 2024 VORC is a free training, practice, and competition program on robotics for high school students nationwide with the aim of sharing knowledge, creating opportunities for young people to access design platforms and develop robotics ideas, enhancing their skills and capabilities, while encouraging learning, creativity, and social concern of Vietnamese students.

Notably, The top 8 teams will receive a kit to participate in FIRST Tech Challenge Vietnam in Hà Nội in February 2025.

Who can join?

  • Students from high schools across the country. Team members must be high school students at the time the program is held (from July 1, 2024) and that member must be on the list of members confirmed by the school. For 10th grade students in the 24-25 school year, these members can still participate but must be on the list confirmed by the school.

  • Each high school can have from 01 to 03 teams representing the school to participate in the program.

  • Each team must have at least 01 mentor to support the team in terms of expertise and procedures.

  • Each team must have at least 3 members and a maximum of 15 members (each team should have members working in both technical and communication, finance, and external affairs).

After successful registration, teams will receive their team code, this code will represent the team and be used throughout the program.

VORC 2024 Rules

Vietnam Open Robotics Challenge 2024 will be held in 2 rounds: Online training round and Challenge round.

Online training round:
Teams will participate in training programs on mechanical design, programming, and electronics. In addition, teams also have the opportunity to access and learn more about topics such as communication, team management, and interviews with the judges. At the end of the training process, teams submit their result to the organizers for evaluation.
Detail of the Online training round can be found here

Challenge round:
In the Challenge round, teams will prepare technical documents, perform communication challenges, and participate in interviews with the judges. The Challenge round topic will be announced by the organizers on July 29, 2024.

Register for VORC 2024

Step 1: Finish the registration form here
(Registration time: 10/06/2024 - 23h59' 30/06/2024).
Teams can register the team name first to confirm participation in the program and add members later (Teams add members by filling out the form again before 05/07/2024).

Step 2:
100% team members need to join Discord VORC 2024. Any member not joining the Discord (after 07/07/2024) will not be qualify as an official team members.

Step 3:
Teams need to submit the school's confirmation letter to register for the program. Details at Event summary.

Step 4:
Teams need to anwser the entry survey before 23h59’ 20/07/2024
Requirement: 100% team members and mentors submitted the survey.

If the team has any changes in team information and member list, the team needs to send information to the organizers via email: robotics@fe.edu.vn
If the team has any questions about the registration process, the team can ask questions through the fanpage, email or VORC Discord
