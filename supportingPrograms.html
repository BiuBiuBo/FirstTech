FIRST Tech Challenge
101 Workshop Series

FIRST Tech Challenge 101 Workshop Series, a comprehensive crash course designed to equip FTC Vietnam teams with the crucial knowledge and skills needed to excel in the competition.

The series will cover the following key topics:

  • Team's Journey
  • Building a Team and Mindset for FTC
  • Business and Fundraising
  • CAD and Programming
  • Awards and Judging Interview

Time of workshop: 8:00 PM PST Saturday, 11:00 AM ICT Sunday
Duration: 1 hour
Minimum and maximum 2 teams per workshop.
We also welcome proposals on other topics!
